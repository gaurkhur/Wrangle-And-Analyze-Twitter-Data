INSIGHTS GATHERED FROM TWITTER ARCHIVES FOR @dog_rates¶

Insight 1¶

Software platform chosen for tweets¶

Of the 2278 cleaned tweets that are present in the merged dataset (df_twitter), most of the tweets have been posted/rated from twitter app on iphone in the years 2015, 2016, and 2017.

Insight 2¶

Breeds with highest and lowest average ratings¶

Of the 113 different breeds in the dataset, Bouvier Des Flandres, Saluki, Briard and Tibetian Mastiff have the highest ratings; while Scotch Terrier, Walker Hound, Soft-Coated Wheaten Terrier, Japanese Spaniel breeds have the lowest ratings.

Insight 3¶

Breeds with highest favorite and retweet count¶

Of the 113 breeds- Golden retriever, Labrador Retriever, Pembroke and Chihuahua have the highest number of cumulative favrorite count and highest number of retweets.
